Doctor's Profile



Dr Bukoye, C. G.

Obstetrics & Gynaecology
Port Harcourt, Nigeria

Dr Bukoye obtained a bachelor of medicine; bachelor of surgery (mbbs) at the university of dundee(uk).
she had her residency training in obstetrics and gynaecology in the uk following which she became a fellow of the national postgraduate medical college of nigeria (fmcog) and the west africa college of surgeons (fwacs) in 2013.
presently, she is a senior lecturer and a honorary consultant at excellence university, port harcourt and the university teaching hospital, port harcourt, rivers state respectively.........
